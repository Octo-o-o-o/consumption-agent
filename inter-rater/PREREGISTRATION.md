## Leakage screening (hygiene procedure, completed before freeze)

Per the protocol's screening rule, the case set passed a naive-reader leakage screening before freezing: a first panel (2026-07-14), a targeted first-sentence rewrite with zero factual changes verified by adversarial review (2026-07-15), and a re-screening with a fresh naive panel under a pre-registered adjudication rule (2026-07-16, rule frozen before any re-screening data). Screening materials, raw answer sheets, scoring scripts, and the full adjudication report are released with the study data. This is reported as a design-hygiene procedure, not as a statistical claim of "no leakage."
